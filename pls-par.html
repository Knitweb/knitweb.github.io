PLS & PAR — two coins, one web

Every unit that will ever exist is minted by the protocol as a bounded reward for something a peer independently verified: PLS for useful computation, PAR for confirmed observations of the physical world. No premine, no admin mint, integer-exact on every machine.

PLS — proof of useful work

Escrow-funded jobs — GPU vision, quantum path sampling (PQ), chemistry validation, relay, storage — verified by sampled re-execution. Fraud settles nothing. Reward ≤ escrow spent, hard supply caps, replay-proof coinbases.

PAR — proof of observation

A bounty names a place, an object class and a time window; a Quest 3S, phone or browser answers it with a confirmed, signed observation peers re-check. Raw captures stay in your personal pod — the ledger sees only signed facts.

The launch faucets

150 granted places per country per coin, a fair FIFO waitlist beyond that, one self-custody pls1 address per person. The faucet stores no personal data: an address, a country code, a timestamp — nothing else.

Your GPU joins only by consent: using the Pulse wallet grants the web at most 49% of your device — a signed, scoped, one-tap-revocable record, never a majority.
